Live Music Nights

At Big Brass Bar, every night is a celebration of the vibrant and soulful sounds of live jazz. Our Live Music Nights offer an intimate and electrifying experience, featuring an eclectic mix of performances from renowned jazz musicians and emerging local talent. From sultry saxophone solos to the powerful sounds of brass ensembles, we bring the best of jazz to our stage, setting the perfect mood for an unforgettable evening.
